REMOTE WORK POLICY

Eligibility:
All full-time employees who have completed 90 days of employment are eligible for remote work.

Schedule:
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must be available during core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM
- Flexible schedule outside core hours

Equipment:
- Company provides laptop and necessary software
- Employees responsible for reliable internet connection
- IT support available 24/7 via helpdesk

Performance Standards:
- Same productivity expectations as in-office work
- Weekly check-ins with direct manager required
- Quarterly performance reviews

Communication:
- Respond to emails/messages within 2 hours during core hours
- Keep calendar updated with availability
- Attend all required meetings (virtual or in-person)

LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT POLICY

Training Budget:
Each employee receives annual professional development budget:
- Individual Contributors: $2,000 per year
- Managers: $3,000 per year
- Senior Leaders: $5,000 per year

Eligible Expenses:
- Online courses and certifications (Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning)
- Conference attendance and registration fees
- Professional books and learning materials
- External training workshops
- Professional coaching sessions

Time Off for Learning:
- Up to 40 hours per year for professional development
- Additional time approved for job-relevant certifications
- Must coordinate with manager in advance

Certification Reimbursement:
- Company covers exam fees for job-relevant certifications
- Completion bonus: $500-$2,000 based on certification level
- Examples: AWS, PMP, Six Sigma, CPA, etc.

PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS

Review Cycle:
- Annual performance reviews: December
- Mid-year check-ins: June
- Quarterly one-on-one meetings with manager

Performance Ratings:
1. Needs Improvement - Below expectations
2. Meets Expectations - Satisfactory performance
3. Exceeds Expectations - Above average performance
4. Outstanding - Exceptional performance
5. Exceptional - Top tier performance

Review Components:
- Goal Achievement: 40% weight
- Core Competencies: 30% weight
- Leadership & Collaboration: 20% weight
- Innovation & Initiative: 10% weight

PROMOTION CRITERIA

General Requirements:
- Minimum 12 months in current role
- Meets or exceeds performance expectations consistently
- Demonstrates readiness for next level responsibilities
- Manager and skip-level endorsement required

Career Levels:

Individual Contributor Track:
- IC1: Entry Level (0-2 years experience)
- IC2: Intermediate (2-4 years experience)
- IC3: Senior (4-7 years experience)
- IC4: Staff (7-10 years experience)
- IC5: Principal (10+ years experience)

Management Track:
- M1: Team Lead (2-5 direct reports)
- M2: Manager (5-10 direct reports)
- M3: Senior Manager (manages managers)
- M4: Director (strategic department leadership)
- M5: VP/Executive (organization-level leadership)

Promotion Process:
1. Self-nomination or manager recommendation
2. Promotion packet preparation with evidence
3. Peer review and feedback collection
4. Calibration committee review
5. Final approval from leadership

Timeline:
- Promotion reviews: Bi-annual (June & December)
- Effective date: 30 days after approval
